# 70+ Engineering Blogs To Follow in 2025.

Many people ask me where I learn or if I find inspiration to write, and one part of the answer is some excellent engineering blogs I follow regularly. In this article, we will see some great blogs I read daily and others I read occasionally.

Also, I will share a complete list of 400+ engineering blogs with you, which you can track and read.

So, let’s see which one I like the most.

---

Generally, I follow some blogs regularly and others not, but when I find good articles there. Here are the best ones.

## My favorite engineering blogs (top 10)

- **[LinkedIn Engineering](https://engineering.linkedin.com/)** - Learn about scalable systems, AI-driven personalization, and real-world applications of cutting-edge technologies in professional networking.
- **[Engineering at Meta](https://engineering.fb.com/)** - Explore the tech behind Meta’s ecosystem, from advanced machine learning to immersive AR/VR experiences.
- **[Dropbox Tech Blog](https://dropbox.tech/)** - Gain insights into simplifying productivity through cloud infrastructure, storage optimization, and engineering efficiency.
- **[Spotify Engineering](https://engineering.atspotify.com/)** - Discover how Spotify delivers personalized audio experiences through data engineering, machine learning, and innovative backend systems.
- **[Netflix Tech Blog](https://netflixtechblog.com/)** - Dive into the engineering behind Netflix’s streaming platform, from high-performance microservices to content delivery at scale.
- **[GitHub Engineering](https://github.blog/engineering/)** - Find how GitHub builds collaborative tools and improves developer workflows with a focus on reliability and performance.
- **[Stripe Blog](https://stripe.com/blog)** - Learn about building reliable, scalable payment systems and APIs that power internet businesses globally.
- **[Uber Engineering Blog](https://www.uber.com/en-RS/blog/engineering/)** - Explore the challenges and solutions in building mobility, logistics, and global scale systems.
- **[Slack Engineering Blog](https://slack.engineering/)** - Understand how Slack creates seamless collaboration through robust infrastructure and user-centric design.
- **[Engineering@Microsoft](https://devblogs.microsoft.com/engineering-at-microsoft/)** and **[Microsoft Developer Blogs](https://devblogs.microsoft.com/)** -****Microsoft's blogs provide updates on AI, cloud computing, and software development tools. They also share best practices and innovations that enhance productivity for developers working on diverse projects.

Along with my favorite blogs, which I tend to read almost daily, I also read other great blogs occasionally.
