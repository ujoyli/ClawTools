# Why I'm starting a newsletter

*And what you can do to support it right now*

**This is the Tech World With Milan Newsletter**, a newsletter about Everything in the beautiful world of Software Engineering and Technology.

[![](images/162284e4-e9e3-4b8f-9417-ac64d99ea7cb_1100x220.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!iu5u!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F162284e4-e9e3-4b8f-9417-ac64d99ea7cb_1100x220.png)

After a long time and probably more than one thousand LinkedIn, Blog, and Twitter posts, I decided to go with a proper **Newsletter**. And here is the main reason: many people asked me to do so because LinkedIn posts are not optimal for writing because you cannot bookmark them properly. There is a hard limit on the length, so I needed to cut 90% of my posts, and also, some posts are likely to be missed in this way. Now, you will **receive them directly in your inbox**.

Yes, what I liked is with LinkedIn and Twitter, that you're constrained with your writing, so everything we write needs to be simplified and digested. So this will remain the same with the newsletter.

The Newsletter will not be weekly or bi-weekly, with will be **irregular**. This means I won’t bother you, but I can hit your inbox only with **highly relevant and high-quality posts** in a shorter form, while I will continue to write on social networks too. What you will find there are some of my **most reacted posts**, now enriched, but also some **new stuff**.

I will write about the following:

- **Topic and trends** in the software industry.
- Everything about **Software architecture and programming**.
- How to have a **great career in tech**.
- How to **build and motivate teams**.
- I am working with and **coaching people to grow**.
- Book **recommendations**.
- Deep dive into **Cloud, DevOps, .NET, SQL**.
- And more…
